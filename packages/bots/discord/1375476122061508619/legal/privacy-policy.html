Privacy Policy

WhiteOut Survival CRM Discord Bot

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the WhiteOut Survival CRM Discord Bot ("the Bot", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our services.

Important: By using the Bot, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Data

Data Type Purpose Retention Period
Discord User ID User identification and verification Until user removes bot access
Discord Username Display and communication purposes Until user removes bot access
Server (Guild) ID Server-specific configurations Until bot is removed from server
Channel IDs Message routing and feature functionality Until bot is removed from server
Message Timestamps Activity tracking and event scheduling 30 days

2.2 User-Provided Data

Data Type Purpose Retention Period
Game ID Player verification and alliance management Until user requests deletion
Alliance Name Alliance organization and role assignment Until user leaves alliance or requests deletion
Alliance Role (R1-R5) Permission management and features access Until role changes or user requests deletion
Language Preference Localized user experience Until user changes preference
Event Information Event scheduling and reminders Until event completion or deletion

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Uses

  • User Verification: Confirming player identity using game API
  • Alliance Management: Organizing users into alliance groups
  • Role Assignment: Providing appropriate Discord roles and permissions
  • Event Management: Scheduling events and sending reminders
  • Localization: Providing content in user's preferred language
  • Server Statistics: Generating anonymous usage statistics

3.2 Communication

We use your information to:

  • Send event reminders and notifications
  • Provide alliance-specific announcements
  • Deliver moderator messages and gift codes
  • Respond to user commands and interactions

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored in:

  • MongoDB Database: Encrypted and secured with access controls
  • Server Memory: Temporary data during active sessions
  • Log Files: Error logs and system monitoring (anonymized)

4.2 Security Measures

  • Database encryption at rest and in transit
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • No storage of sensitive authentication tokens

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

5.1 Third-Party Services

The Bot integrates with the following services:

  • Discord API: For bot functionality and user interaction
  • WhiteOut Survival API: For player verification (if available)
  • MongoDB Atlas: For data storage and management

5.2 Data Sharing Policy

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes
  • Provide your information to other users without consent

We MAY share information:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights and safety of users
  • With your explicit consent

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your stored data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Export your data in a readable format
  • Withdrawal: Revoke consent for data processing
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing activities

6.1 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights:

  • Use the /privacy command in Discord (if available)
  • Contact us through our support channels
  • Remove the bot from your server (for server owners)
  • Leave servers where the bot is active

7. Data Retention

7.1 Retention Periods

  • Active Users: Data retained while bot is in use
  • Inactive Users: Data deleted after 90 days of inactivity
  • Event Data: Deleted 7 days after event completion
  • Log Data: Deleted after 30 days

7.2 Automatic Deletion

Data is automatically deleted when:

  • A user leaves all servers with the bot
  • The bot is removed from a server
  • A user explicitly requests data deletion
  • Retention periods expire

8. Cookies and Tracking

The Bot does not use cookies or tracking technologies. All interactions occur through Discord's platform using their native API.

9. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information during international transfers.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of significant changes through:

  • Bot announcements in Discord servers
  • Updates to this document with revision dates
  • Direct messages to server administrators

12. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Consent: When you voluntarily provide information
  • Legitimate Interest: For bot functionality and security
  • Contract Performance: To provide requested services
  • Legal Obligation: When required by law
